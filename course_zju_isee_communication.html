  • 输出信噪比 \[ (SNR)_{out}=\frac{E[m_{out}^2(t)]}{E[n_{out}^2(t)]} \]

4.1 DSB-SC AM

  • 输入噪声

\[ P_u=E[\frac 1 2 m^2(t)]\\ P_{n_{in}}=B\cdot N_0 \]

  • 相干解调 \[ r(t)=[m(t)+n_c(t)]\cos\tp f_c t-n_s(t)\sin \tp f_c t\\ r(t)\cos\tp f_c t=\frac 1 2[m(t)+n_c(t)]+高频 \]

how to derive?

经过低通滤波后

  • 输出 \[ P_{m_{out}}=\frac 1 4 E[m^2(t)]\\ P_{n_{out}}=\frac 1 4E[n_c^2(t)]=\frac 1 4 N_0B \]

  • 信噪比 \[ G=2 \]

4.2 SSB AM

  • 输入

\[ P_u=\frac 1 2\bra{E[m^2(t)+\h m^2(t)]}\\ =E[m^2(t)]\\ P_{n_{in}}=B\cdot N_0 \]

B仅为双边带的一半

  • 相对于带宽相同的未调制信号来讲

\[ (SNR)_{in}=\frac{E[m^2(t)]}{BN_0} \]

  • 相干解调 \[ r(t)\cos\tp f_c t=\frac 1 2[m(t)+n_c(t)]+高频项 \]

  • 输出 \[ P_{m_{out}}=\frac 1 4E[m^2(t)]\\ P_{n_{out}}=\frac 14 E[n_c^2(t)]=\frac 1 4 BN_0\\ (SNR)_{in}=\frac{E[m^2(t)]}{BN_0} \]

  • 信噪比 \[ G=1 \] 单边带和抑制载波双边带的输出信噪比是一样的

Why

4.3 普通AM调制

  • 输入 \[ u(t)=[1+am_n(t)]\cos(\tp f_ct)\\ P_u=\frac 1 2 +\frac {a^2}2E\lrbra[]{m_n^2(t)}\\ P_{n_{in}}=N_0B\\ (SNR)_{in}=\frac{P_u}{N_0B} \] ​

  • 相干解调 \[ r(t)\cos\tp f_c t=\frac 1 2[1+am_n(t)+n_c(t)]+高频项\\ m_{out}=\frac a 2m_n(t)\\ n_{out}=\frac 1 2 n_c(t)\\ (SNR)_{out}=\frac{a^2E\lrbra[]{m_n^2(t)}}{N_0B} \] > 输出不是\(\frac 1 2+\frac a 2 m_n(t)\)

\[ G=\frac{2a^2E[m_n^2(t)]}{1+a^2E[m_n^2(t)]} \]

  • 包络检波

输入(已调信号加噪声) \[ r(t)=[1+am_n(t)+n_c(t)]\cos\tp f_c t-n_s(t)\sin \tp f_c t\\ =V(t)\cos[\tp f_c t+\phi(t)]\\ V(t)=\sqrt{[1+am_n(t)+n_c(t)]^2+n_s^2(t)} \] \(1+a_m(t)\gg n_c(t),n_s(t)\) \[ V(t)\approx 1+am_n(t)+n_c(t) \] 仅差一个因子0.5 不影响输出信噪比
