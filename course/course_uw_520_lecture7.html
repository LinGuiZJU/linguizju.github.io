1 Smoothing \(\h S\kd(\cdot)\)

1.1 idea

average \(\h S\kd(\cdot)\) across frequencies

1 make sense if \(S(\cdot)\) slowly varying \[ S(f_{k-M})\approx \cdots\approx S(f_k)\approx\cdots\approx S(f_{k+M}) \]

1.2 average-smoothing window

\[ \overline S(f_k)\triangleq \frac 1{2M+1}\sumsym j M \h S^{(p)}(f_{k-j})\\ \h S^{(ds)}(f'_k)\triangleq \sumsym j M g_j\h S\kd(f_{k-j}'),f_k'\triangleq \frac{k}{N'\dt} \]

1.3 Lag Window

typically \(N'\geq N\) \[ \h S^{(lw)}(f)\triangleq \intsym{f_N}V_m(f-\phi)\h S^{(d)}(\phi)\d \phi\\ =\dt\sumsym \tau {(N-1)}v_{m,\tau}\st\kd \etpin{f\tau\dt}\\ v_{m,\tau}\leftrightarrow V_m(\cdot)\\ w_{m,\tau}\triangleq\equtwo{v_{m,\tau}}{\abs\tau<N}{0}{\abs\tau\geq N}\\ \bra{w_{m,\tau}}\leftrightarrow W_m(\cdot) \]

\(V_m(\cdot)\), \(2f_N\) periodic, typical shapes: Gaussian PDF or rectangular PDF \(W_m(\cdot)\) is a standardized version of \(V_m(\cdot)\)

\[ \bra{\st^{(lw)}}\leftrightarrow \h S^{(lw)}(\cdot)\\ \st^{(lw)}\triangleq\equtwo{w_{m,\tau}\st\kd}{\abs\tau\leq N-1}{0}{\abs\tau\geq N} \] filtering \(\h S\kd\) to extract a ‘signal’ buried in substantial noise

1.4 relationship between $S^{(ds)} $and \(\h S^{(lw)}\)

\[ \h S^{(ds)}(f_k')=\dt\sumsym\tau{(N-1)}v_{g,\tau}\st\kd\etpin{f_k'\tau\dt}\\ v_{g,\tau}\triangleq\sumsym j M g_j\etpi{j\tau/N} \] all \(\h S^{(ds)}\) can be expressed as \(\h S^{(ls)}\) \[ \h S^{(lw)}(f'_k)=\sumsym j {(N-1)}g_j\h S\kd(f'_{k-j})\\ g_j\triangleq \frac 1 {(2N-1)\dt}W_m(f_j')\\ f'_k=\frac k{(2N-1)\dt} \] all \(\h S^{(lw)}(\cdot)\) can be expressed as \(\h S^{(ds)}(\cdot)\), the first avoids convolution and is preferable

\(f_k'\) determine \(f\)

1.5 Conditions

  • \(\intsym {f_N}W_m(f)\d f=1\Leftrightarrow w_{m,0}=1\Leftrightarrow \sumsym j{(N-1)}g_j=1\)
  • as \(m\rightarrow \infty, W_m(\cdot)\) acts like a $$ function
  • Sufficient(but not necessary ) for \(\h S^{(lw)}(f)\geq 0\): \(W_m(f)\geq 0\)

1.6 Smoothing Window Bandwidth

\[ \b_W\triangleq \lrbra(){12\intsym {f_N}f^2W_m(f)\d f}^{1/2}\\ =\frac 1 \dt \lrbra(){1+\frac {12}{\pi^2}\sumsubtri \tau 1 {N-1} \frac{(-1)^\tau}{\tau^2}w_{m,\tau}}^{1/2} \]

  • 12 gives rectangular \(W_m\) its natural width
  • can be imaginary when \(W_m(f)<0\) for some \(f\)

second bandwidth measure \[ B_W\triangleq \frac 1{\intsym{f_N}W_m^2(f)\d f}\\ =\dfrac 1{\dt\sumsym\tau{(N-1)}w_{m,\tau}^2} \]

2.1 expectation

\[ E\bra{\h S^{(lw)}(f)}=\intsym f_N \mathcal U_m(f-\phi)S(\phi)\d \phi\\ \mathcal U_m(f)=\intsym f_N W_m(f-f'')\mathcal H(f'')\d f'' \] 2nd expression \[ E\bra{\h S^{(lw)}(f)}=\dt \sumsym \tau {(N-1)}\lrbra (){w_{m,\tau}\st\sumsubtri t 0 {N-\abs\tau-1}h_{t+\abs\tau}h_t}\etpin{f\tau\dt}\\ \mathcal U_m(f)=\dt \sumsym \tau {N-1}w_{m,\tau}(\sumsubtri t 0{N-\abs\tau - 1}h_th_{t+\abs\tau})\etpin{f\tau\dt} \] compare \[ E\bra{\h S\kd(f)}=\intsym {f_N}\mathcal H(f-\phi)S(\phi)\d \phi \]

2.2 Bias

\[ b_W(f)\triangleq \intsym{f_N}W_m(f-f') S(f')\d f'-S(f)\\ =\frac{S''(f)}2\intsym {f_N}\phi^2W_m(\phi)\d \phi=\frac{S''(f)}{24}\b_W^2 \]

3 2nd Moment Properties

3.1 assumpations

  • \(\h S\kd(f'_j)\)’s pairwise uncorrelated over \(f'_j=\frac j{N'\dt}\)

  • \(J\) such that \(2f_J'\approx \t{bandwidth of } W_m(\cdot)\)

    bandwidth of \(W_m(\cdot)\)?

  • keeping away from 0 and Nyquist frequencies

  • \(S(\cdot)\) slowly varying over \([f_k'-f_J', f_k'+f_J']\)

3.2 approx

\[ \h S^{(lw)}(f_k')\approx\sumsym j J W_m(f'_j)\h S\kd(f_j'+f_k')\frac 1{N'\dt}\\ \var\bra{\h S^{(lw)}(f'_k)}\approx \frac{S^2(f_k')}{N'\dt}\intsym{f_N}W_m^2(\phi)\d \phi\approx \frac{C_h S^2(f)}{B_WN\dt}\\ N'\approx N/C_h, C_h=N\sumsubtri t 0{N-1}h_t^4 \]
