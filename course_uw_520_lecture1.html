  • Nonparametric Estimation of \(S_j\)

    \(S_j=var\{A_j\}=var\{B_j\}\)$

    • yields \[ A_j=\frac 2 N\sumsub{t=0}{N-1}X_t\cos(2\pi f_jt)\\ B_j=\frac 2 N\sumsub{t=0}{N-1}X_t\sin(2\pi f_j t) \]

    • \(\hat S_j\) \[ \hat S_j\triangleq\frac {A_j^2+B_j^2}{2} \]

    • assumption of \(S_j\) \[ S_j(\alpha,\beta)=\frac \beta{1+\alpha^2-2\alpha\cos(2\pi f_j)} \]

      Why this assumption?

      estimate \[ \hat S_j(\hat\alpha,\hat\beta)=\frac {\hat\beta}{1+\hat\alpha^2-2\hat\alpha\cos(2\pi f_j)} \]
